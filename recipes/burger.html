Description

A burger is a popular sandwich consisting of a cooked patty placed inside a sliced bun along with vegetables, cheese, and sauces.

It is quick to make, customizable, and loved worldwide as a classic comfort food.

Ingredients

  • Burger buns
  • Ground beef or veg patty
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato slices
  • Cheese slice
  • Onion rings
  • Ketchup or mayo

Steps

  1. Heat a pan or grill.
  2. Cook the patty until fully done.
  3. Lightly toast the buns.
  4. Place lettuce and tomato on bottom bun.
  5. Add cooked patty and cheese.
  6. Add onions and sauce.
  7. Cover with top bun and serve.
