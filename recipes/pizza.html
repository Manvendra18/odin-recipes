Description

Pizza is a popular Italian dish consisting of a flat bread base topped with tomato sauce, cheese, and various toppings.

It is baked in an oven until the crust is crispy and the cheese is melted.

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Bell peppers
  • Olives
  • Onions

  6. Serve hot.
